About us

We are a small group of designers aming to become a industry leader in design of rain clothing and hiking clothes. Our idea started with our group going on hiking trips and not being satisfied with the quality of other brands. Now we proudly make clothes for all people that feel the same as we do! Our products is working in a huge variety of weather, so you can use it in places like windy mountains, or rainy valleys, our product even keeps the right temperature on a hot summer day!
